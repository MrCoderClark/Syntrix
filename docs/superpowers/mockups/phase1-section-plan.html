10 / 10

Voting & feeds

feat/voting-feeds full-stack ~2 days

Ships: upvote / downvote on posts + comments. Postgres triggers maintain score. Hot / New / Top feed sorts on the home page and per-community page. The "your feed" view from the home mockup goes live.

DBsyntrix.post_votes, syntrix.comment_votes; triggers; functional index for the Hot formula.
AlgoHot = log10(max(abs(score), 1)) * sign(score) + (created_at - epoch) / 45000 (Reddit-style). Functional index makes this fast.
AcceptanceVote / un-vote / flip-vote works; counter triggers stay accurate under concurrency (pgbench-style test); Hot feed orders sensibly across recent + popular.

Cross-cutting concerns

Permissions grow incrementally

There is no standalone "permissions section." app/auth/perm.py starts in Section 4 with current_user and the first guard. Each subsequent section adds the functions it needs:

  • 06 communities → can_create_community_request, can_join, can_ban, can_rename_community, can_approve_request
  • 08 posts → can_create_post, can_edit_post, can_remove_post, can_pin_post
  • 09 comments → can_comment, can_edit_comment, can_remove_comment
  • 10 voting → can_vote (with self-vote guard)

Rate limits arrive in 04 and extend per section

The rate_limit_buckets table + atomic refill+take helper land in Section 4 (auth — login is the most pressure-sensitive surface). Each subsequent section adds its own limit keys:

  • 06 → community_request:user:<uuid> (2/day)
  • 07 → upload_sign:user:<uuid> (100/hr)
  • 08 → post_create:user:<uuid> (with new-account belt)
  • 09 → comment_create:user:<uuid> (60/hr)
  • 10 → vote:user:<uuid> (600/hr)

Critical-path summary

Hard dependencies (cannot reorder):

  • 01 → 02 → 03 → 04 — chassis before auth
  • 04 → 06 — communities need signed-in users
  • 06 → 08 — posts need a community to live in
  • 07 → 08 (image embeds depend on the upload pipeline)
  • 08 → 09 — comments need posts
  • 08 → 10 (votes on posts), 09 → 10 (votes on comments)

Soft dependencies (can shift):

  • 05 profiles can slot anywhere after 04. I've placed it before 06 so community pages can link to real profiles.
  • 07 storage can land any time after 04. Placed before 08 so posts can use it directly.

Total estimated Phase 1 wall time: 18 working days (with you in the loop reviewing & merging each PR). Real-world ~3–4 weeks of evenings/weekends. Plus the polish backlog from PRD.md §"Polish backlog" — landed alongside the relevant section, not deferred to a separate cycle.
